## Key Claims (Computationally Testable via RAST/BV-BRC)
1. RAST revealed 2 putative iron acquisition subsystems across all 4 strains
2. Subsystem 1: Iron siderophore sensor and receptor system 
3. Subsystem 2: Heme, hemin uptake and utilization systems / hemin transport system
4. DyP-type peroxidase and ferritin-like protein oligomers only in K279a
5. FUR present across all 4 strains
6. 17 functional targets identified (Table 2)
7. Specific locus tags for K279a (Table 2)
