# Jiang et al. 2017 - Paper Notes

## Key Numbers
- 57 experimentally validated Streptomyces ARG proteins (from ARDB + CARD)
- 39/57 have self-protecting roles or are in biosynthesis clusters
- 12/57 show phylogenetic evidence of interphylum HGT (actinobacteria → proteobacteria)
- 7/12 have proteobacterial proteins more similar to actinobacteria than any other phyla
- 2 potential RECENT transfers: Cmx and LmrA
- Figure 1 shows the 12 HGT pairs with identities
