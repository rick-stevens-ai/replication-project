# Yuan et al. 2019 - Paper Notes
## blaNDM-5 carried by a hypervirulent Klebsiella pneumoniae with sequence type 29
- DOI: 10.1186/s13756-019-0596-1
- PMID: 31452874
- PMC: PMC6701021
- Journal: Antimicrob Resist Infect Control, 8:133 (2019)

## Key Quantitative Claims

### Genome Assembly
1. Draft genome assembled into 29 contigs (28 >1000 bp)
2. Total genome size: 5,474,953 bp
3. GC content: 57.29%

### MLST & Typing
4. ST29 (allele profile: gapA-2, infB-3, mdh-2, pgi-3, phoE-6, rpoB-4, tonB-4)
5. Capsular type: wzi115-K54

### Resistance
6. blaNDM-5 is the only carbapenemase gene (on plasmid pNDM5-SCNJ1)
7. Chromosomal resistance genes: blaSHV-187, oqxA, oqxB, fosA
8. Imipenem MIC >256 μg/ml; Meropenem MIC >256 μg/ml (wet-lab)
9. Colistin MIC 2 μg/ml (susceptible; wet-lab)

### Plasmid pNDM5-SCNJ1
10. 45,255-bp IncX3 plasmid
11. GC content: 46.83%
12. 100% coverage and 99.99% identity with pNDM_MGR194 (KF220657)
13. No other resistance genes besides blaNDM-5
14. Conjugative (transfer freq 10^-6; wet-lab)

### Virulence Plasmid pVir-SCNJ1
15. 211,807-bp plasmid (IncHI1/IncFIB-type)
16. Carries rmpA, iutA, iucABCD, iroBCDN
17. rmpA2 truncated (frameshift mutation, internal stop codon)
18. 99.71% identical to pLVPK (NC_005249) at 93% coverage
19. 99% coverage and 99.99% identity to pL22-1 (NZ_CP031258)

### Virulence Genes (Chromosomal + Plasmid)
20. rmpA (mucoid phenotype regulator) - on pVir-SCNJ1
21. Aerobactin: iucABCD, iutA - on pVir-SCNJ1
22. Salmochelin: iroBCDEN - partly on pVir-SCNJ1
23. Yersiniabactin: fyuA, irp1, irp2, ybtAEPQSTUX - chromosomal
24. Type 3 fimbriae: mrkABCDFHIJ - chromosomal
25. Enterobactin: entABCDEFS, fepABCDG - chromosomal

### Phylogenetic Analysis
26. 60 ST29 K. pneumoniae genomes used (59 from GenBank + SCNJ1)
27. SCNJ1 closest to SCLZ15-011 (GCA_001630805) with 198 SNPs difference
28. blaNDM-5 described for first time in ST29

### IncX3 Plasmid Phylogeny
29. 230 IncX3 plasmids analyzed
30. FastTree used for species tree from concatenated MSA (OrthoFinder)

### Wet-lab Claims (NOT TESTABLE in silico)
- String test: 35 mm (hypermucoviscous)
- G. mellonella survival: 0% with SCNJ1 at 1×10^5 cfu/mL at 72h
- Control strains: 56.2% (KPLZ1050) and 50.0% (KPNJ2) survival
- Conjugation frequency: 10^-6 transconjugant/recipient
